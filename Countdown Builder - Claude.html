1
Open your countdown in CodePen

CodePen is free and works directly in Canva's embed element. Click the button — a new tab opens with your timer pre-loaded.

2
Save the pen

In the CodePen tab, press Ctrl+S (or ⌘S) to save. No account needed — it saves as an anonymous pen.

3
Copy the URL & paste into Canva

Copy the CodePen URL from your browser. In Canva, go to Apps → Embed, paste the URL, and click Add.
